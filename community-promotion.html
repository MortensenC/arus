Have your next digital project built for free!

by Patrick
So whats the catch you may ask?…

No catch, this is a great way to get to know our community and have fun in the process!

Submit your mobile app, web app or website needs, and have your friends vote you to the top... it's that simple. Your social currency is worth money. The friends you have on Facebook that want to promote your idea. They can vote for the best idea submitted. It's pretty simple. The more they like your idea the more social currency you will obtain. (they need to have liked the Ahoy-bvi page and to have liked your idea that we have posted on the Ahoy-bvi page)  
On October 2, 2017 the results will be totaled and the winner of the contest will receive $2,500 worth of web or mobile app development from Ahoy-bvi absolutely free. The announcement of winners will be on Facebook live (for those unable to attend in person) at de loose mongoose bar! Ahoy-bvi will also have an open bar tab (up to $200 total collectively ) giving free drinks for attendees!

“Your social currency is worth money”
Ok, so how do I get started you may ask ?

  • Like the Ahoy-bvi Facebook page 
  • Submit your idea on the form below.
  • Invite all of your friends to like the Ahoy-bvi page so they can vote for your idea 
  • Keep an eye out for when we post your idea. 
  • Have your friends like your idea by clicking the like button on your concept posted on our page
  • Win!!
(shares don't count, neither do fake accounts... let's keep it real guys, also serious ideas only please...try and refrain from apps that summon aliens for world domination, or time machines)
