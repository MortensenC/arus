Forge BVI & Ahoy-bvi technology training collaboration

by Patrick
Education

Ahoy-bvi has partnered with Forge BVI on making technology education available as a part of their program. Ahoy-bvi is a firm believer in the phrase “if you teach a man to fish” proverb. We are happy to announce that we will be providing entry level training on:

Website and internet/digital media fundamentals:
  • www and the web 101
  • Domain registartion
  • Servers 101
  • Database 101
  • Email marketing
  • SEO search engine optimization basics
Market analysis fundamentals:
  • What is your target market (how can you find them)
  • Content targeting and research best practices
  • Local, Regional and international reach
Business document fundamentals:
  • Microsoft office/ Google docs training
  • Power point/ google sheets
  • Word / google docs
  • Excel / google sheets
“Teach a man to fish”

The Forge is a collaborative training center and workspace for entrepreneurs focused on learning, growing and transforming the economy of the BVI. Forge is a partnership program to MWM global holdings, Ministry of Finance Government of the Virgin Islands, and Boosting Caribbean OCT Enterprises (COSME).
