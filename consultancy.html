Solving large problems is a tough nut to crack. Lets face it; technology is hard, and ever changing. Migrations of legacy systems, data integrity needing to adapt to contemporary patterns.. the list goes on and on.
Ahoy-bvi takes a holistic-organic approach at solving issues by treating them as opportunities in disguise. No matter the size, they all start with a foundational 5 step process. Think of this a tangled ball of yarn, our 5 step methodology are tools to loosen the tangled yarn and start laying things out to be re-woven.

High and Low level opportunities will both be teased out during these phases, this will shine a light on the need for technical consulting or education.

This then breaks off into 2 major paths:

  • High level is more of a consultant role and will be more embedded in the process working daily with stakeholders on mission critical objectives
  • Low level is more a change to educate/train and help adopt best practices.
