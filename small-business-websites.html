Website or Web app

The reason we delineate between the two types is because they are vastly different in time, scope and cost.

Lets's talk about the difference between a web app and website. (We do both but they are different tiers of engineering) A Web app is something like:
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Airbnb
  • Netflix
The pricing tier for building this kind of infrastructure or not really relevant to the small business in the beginning.
A website on the other hand as most of you know, is basically a digital flyer where people can contact you and get a view into your business by presenting material on:
  • Images/videos
  • Services/specials you offer
  • Contact information
  • Contact forms
  • Maps to your location
